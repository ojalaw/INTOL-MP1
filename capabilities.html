Capabilities

"Providing customers with the vital intelligence for operations in vulnerable locations around the world and ensuring that customers are aware of risks/vulnerabilities and kept as safe as possible."

IMINT

IMINT stands for imagery intelligence. This is the exploitation of satellite imagery using specialist techniques taught at a very high level. Imagery intelligence allows us to conduct analysis on an image and identify levels of activty from an overhead perspective. We use multiple different satellites to produce different products including port breakouts, density estimations, vulnerability assessments and many more.

GEOINT

GEOINT stands for Geospatial intelligence. geospatial information to describe, assess and visually depict physical features and geographically referenced activities on the Earth. Geospatial intelligence allows us to create detailed mapping of the Earths surface. We can produce maps at various scales and can also produce route cards, visually appealing maps and many more.

OSINT

OSINT stands for Open-source intelligence This is the collection and analysis of data gathered from open sources to produce actionable intelligence. State of the art training has allowed us to fine tune the ability to search the web for a range of different reasons. We regularly produce Country/Regional profiles for different areas around the world, assessing the political and terror threat in relevant countries.
