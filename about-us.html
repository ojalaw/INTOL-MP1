Our Selection Process

Our analysts come from a variety of different backgrounds. The company was co-founded by ex-military intelligence analysts who have undergone intensive analytical training. All military analysts working for the company have been deployed to highly sensitive environments around the world.

To become part of the team, analysts will endure an intensive selection process to ensure they are up to the required standard. The following criteria are assessed;

Qualifications

Assess relevant qualifications for the job.

Experience

Determine relevant experience for the job

Aptitude

Do the analysts have what it takes

Once the analysts have passed selection, they then move on to the training phase.

Our Training

To become an analyst at INTOL, a general aptitude and basic level of competence has to be displayed prior to joining the team.

The training is broken down into basic intelligence principle training in 3 different stages; basic, intermediate and advanced. Successful completion of this training then enables analysts to progress to capability training.
